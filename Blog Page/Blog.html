Harry Potter and the Prisoner Of Azkaban (Harry Potter #3)

Book Published on July 8, 1999

Blog Published on November 23, 2024

The key plot around which the story pivots is the escape from the notorious prison, Azkaban, of a mass murderer named Sirius Black. Nobody has ever escaped from Azkaban before the the Ministry of Magic is concerned that Sirius will try to get to Hogwarts in order to harm Harry. The reason for this fear is slowly revealed, mostly through overheard conversations. The adults wish to protect the children by not telling them why Sirius would be interested in Harry, but of course they want to know.

Harry Potter and the Order Of Pheonix (Harry Potter #5)

Book Published on June 21, 2003

Blog Published on November 25, 2024

“The Order of the Phoenix” opens with Harry Potter struggling with the emotional aftermath of witnessing the return of Lord Voldemort at the end of the previous book, “Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire.” The Ministry of Magic and the Daily Prophet, the wizarding newspaper, are in denial about Voldemort’s return, painting Harry as a liar and a troublemaker. The story sets the tone for a darker and more mature installment in the series.

Harry Potter and the Half blood Prince (Harry Potter #6)

Book Published on July 16, 2005

Blog Published on November 26, 2024

The story opens with the wizarding world at war after the return of the dark wizard, Lord Voldemort. Harry is prophesied to be the one who will vanquish Voldemort, and his sixth year at Hogwarts sees him receiving lessons from Headmaster Albus Dumbledore as preparation for his epic destiny. The sixth Harry Potter book takes a darker, more grown-up turn than its predecessors, as its hero and his friends navigate the very real throes of adolescence even as they look for a way to save the wizarding world
